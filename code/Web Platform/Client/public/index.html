Meet Pluto and ask it some questions

Who are you?

My name is Pluto and I am from planet Mars.

What are you doing on earth?

I am currently travelling around the galaxy and now I am working with the space exhibition at the Science and Technology Museum. If you want to learn about the galaxy with me you can explore the virtual exhibition.

What is the exhibition about?

The exhibition is about stories, knowledge, and technologies for exploring the cosmos. We have the only fragment of the moon anywhere in Italy!
